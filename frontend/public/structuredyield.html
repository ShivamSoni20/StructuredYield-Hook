Mechanism Design

Four interlocking systems

Each component of the hook serves a distinct role — together they eliminate IL risk and create predictable fixed income.

PT-LP: The Fixed Income Layer

Principal Tokens represent your deposited capital. They are priced at a discount to face value at issuance — the discount encodes the fixed APY. At maturity, PT-LP redeems 1:1 for the underlying LP capital, giving PT holders a guaranteed return regardless of fee volatility.

YT-LP: The Yield Speculation Layer

Yield Tokens capture all swap fees generated by the underlying LP position until maturity. YT-LP is freely tradable — yield speculators can buy exposure to high-fee pools. The afterSwap hook routes 100% of earned fees to YT holders, net of the insurance premium.

IL Insurance Vault

5–10 bps of every deposit flows into a per-pool Insurance Vault. The beforeRemoveLiquidity callback computes the LP's terminal IL using only the pool's internal sqrtPrice — no oracle required. If IL exceeds the fee buffer, the vault covers the shortfall, making PT-LP holders truly whole at maturity.

Dynamic Premium Pricing

The insurance premium is not fixed. The hook tracks recent sqrtPrice delta across the last 50 blocks to estimate realized volatility. Higher volatility = higher premium, which feeds back into the vault. This creates a self-calibrating system where high-risk pools remain solvent without governance or external pricing feeds.

V4 Hook Points

Built on native V4 primitives

No external oracles, no off-chain bots. Every mechanism runs inside Uniswap V4 hook callbacks, transient storage, and the donate() primitive.

beforeAddLiquidity
Records reference sqrtPrice for IL baseline. Mints PT-LP and YT-LP tokens to depositor. Computes premium and routes to Insurance Vault.
afterSwap
Routes fee allocation: X% to YT-LP holders via donate(), Y% to insurance reserve. Updates rolling volatility estimate using sqrtPrice delta.
beforeRemoveLiquidity
Computes terminal IL against reference price. Triggers Insurance Vault payout if IL exceeds threshold. Burns PT-LP and YT-LP balances atomically.
afterRemoveLiquidity
Final settlement: distributes remaining fee buffer to YT holders pro-rata. Emits position close event for off-chain indexers.
donate() primitive
Used by afterSwap to inject fees directly into YT-LP accounting without moving tokens out of the pool — gas-efficient and composable.
transient storage
Stores per-LP reference prices and running IL accumulation within a single transaction, avoiding expensive SSTORE on every swap.
